Look For Friends provides an easy way for your friends to find you in a crowd! This app makes your iPhone screen a specific color so friends can identify you in crowds of people. Simply text a friend your color and hold your phone up high so they can spot you from far away. Look For Friends works best at night or in low light conditions where clothing and other indicators are harder to spot. IFF is also great for concerts, sporting events, and meeting friends in crowded public spaces. Happy hunting!
