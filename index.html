Phone Buddy - BLE Notifier
Watch Bluetooth Notifications
The only Apple Watch app you need. Phone Buddy on Apple Watch alerts you if you lose bluetooth connection with your iPhone and reminds you if you forget your Apple Watch.

Note* An Apple Watch is required to use Phone Buddy - BLE Notifier.

Don't forget your iPhone again using Phone Buddy for Apple Watch!

Phone Buddy is an Apple Watch companion app that keeps track of the connection between your iPhone and Apple Watch. Phone Buddy alerts can be configured to notify you when your iPhone and Apple Watch are moving apart (distance alerts), when devices lose connection (Phone Lost Alert and Watch Left Behind Alert), or when connection is regained (Phone Found Alert, Watch Found Alert).

Keep your iPhone safe and secure using Phone Buddy for Apple Watch Active Bluetooth monitoring.

Phone Buddy is better than phone insurance since it helps prevent a loss! Phone Buddy posts a safety notification on your Apple Watch if you ever lose Bluetooth connection with your iPhone. Enable Phone Distance Alerts and get notified before your Apple Watch loses bluetooth connection with your iPhone.

Phone Buddy is a freemium app.

Phone Buddy *Free* Features:
  • Device Distance Alerts: Get notified on your Apple Watch when your iPhone and Apple Watch are moving apart.
  • Apple Watch Left Behind Alert: Get notified if you forget to bring your Apple Watch.
  • Apple Watch Found Alert: Get notified when your iPhone reconnects with your Apple Watch
  • Bluetooth Signal Strength: Check the Bluetooth signal strength between your Apple Watch and iPhone
  • How-to-use Videos: Phone Buddy on iOS includes how-to-use videos to ensure a smooth setup process for Active Bluetooth Monitoring.
Phone Buddy Premium Features:
  • Phone Lost Alerts: Notifies you on your Apple Watch the moment Bluetooth connection with your iPhone is lost.
  • Phone Found Alerts: Notifies you on your Apple Watch when your phone is found (connection regained by bluetooth finder).
  • Last Connected Map: If your iPhone and Apple Watch disconnect, Phone Buddy on Apple Watch will show a map of the last connected location so you can more easily retrieve your iPhone.
  • Phone Buddy Status Complication: Quickly check if Phone Buddy for Apple Watch is actively monitoring Bluetooth connection right on your favorite Apple Watch Face by placing a Phone Buddy complication. All complication families are supported.
Try it free for 7 days to make sure it works for you.
Phone Buddy is compatible with the following Bluetooth LE enabled watch models running WatchOS 5 or later:

Apple Watch Series 2

Apple Watch Series 3

Apple Watch Series 4

Apple Watch Series 5

Note: An Apple Watch with GPS (location services) and Bluetooth LE (4.0) is required to use Active mode. Phone Buddy uses Bluetooth for notification of disconnection. Phone Buddy uses location to display the last connected location. Phone Buddy uses notifications to let you know if a disconnection occurs. Continued use of GPS running in the background can decrease battery life. Other factors such as the Watch's temperature, battery health, and other apps running in the background can also affect battery performance.
