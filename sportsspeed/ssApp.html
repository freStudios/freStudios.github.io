Phone Buddy Notifier is an Apple Watch app that alerts you when you lose bluetooth connection with your iPhone. Try it now. Apple Watch Series 2+ is required.

Phone Buddy keeps track of your Apple Watch and alerts you when needed. Phone Buddy Alerts can be configured to display when your iPhone connection is lost, when you leave your Apple Watch behind, or when devices move apart! With Phone Buddy Notifier, if you step away from your iPhone, you get a notification on your Apple Watch! It’s that simple.

You can even configure a loud alarm so you know for sure when your devices may be need to retrieved.

Phone Buddy Notifier is the perfect companion app for the Apple Watch with features like Bluetooth Monitoring Alerts, Watch Battery Charged Alert, and Last Connected Map.

Phone Buddy Bluetooth Monitoring Features

  • “Phone Lost” Connection Alert so you never lose your phone again.
  • “Going Away” Distance Alerts with fully customizable bluetooth signal strength thresholds so you keep your devices safe and secure.
  • “Watch Left Behind” Connection Alert so you don’t forget to wear your Apple Watch when you leave your home.
  • “Watch Battery Charged” Alert lets you know when your Apple Watch is ready to go.
  • Phone Safety Alarm plays a loud Alarm when bluetooth connection is lost between your Apple Watch and iPhone to keep everything safe.
  • Last Connected Map to help located iPhone (when network connectivity available) if your devices do disconnect. And more!
